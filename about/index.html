About the Author

My name is Wendy Closson.

In 1998, I was a programming geek who loved weight lifting, anime, and video games.

In 2008, I became a mom – that was really hard!

In 2009, I was diagnosed with a rare cancer – that was much worse!

After a year of experimental treatment, I was physically, emotionally, and mentally broken.

Determined to see my baby grow, I went on a quest to find health.

This quickly spiraled out into a spiritual voyage.

I questioned everything I thought was true.

The answers set me free.
